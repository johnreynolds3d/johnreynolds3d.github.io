Sass, Gulp, FileZilla, and a Bunch of Other Things

18 NOVEMBER 2017

Over the past fortnight I've learned a whole heap of stuff about web development, starting with Sass. I began by going through an excellent tutorial on YouTube — following along using the free trial version of Prepos — before deciding to give Gulp a try. I found an awesome guide on CSS-Tricks, which got me up and running within a couple of hours.

I looked into FTP plugins for VS Code, but after encountering various difficulties, I settled on FileZilla, which seems to have garnered universal acclaim, and evidently for good reason: it's great!
